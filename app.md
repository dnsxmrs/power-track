💡 1. Real-World Problem (START STRONG)
Electricity is one of the biggest operational costs for businesses like hardware stores, warehouses, and small enterprises in the Philippines. However, most businesses only see their electricity consumption through monthly bills or basic monitoring tools.
Even with providers like Meralco, users can only view total consumption, without understanding:
which appliances consume the most
where inefficiencies happen
why electricity usage increases
👉 This creates a gap:
 Businesses cannot control what they cannot clearly understand.
